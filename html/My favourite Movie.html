My favourite Series

I have many series that i like but let's talk about few of the recent favourites that I'd absolutely love to watch over 2-3 times. You can click on the title of the series to be directed to the ott. Let's get started.

  1. Chicago Med

    Chicago med is very good. A series that will keep you very keen on knowing what's gonna happen to the main characters of the show every episode, their personal lifestyles and their professional careers.

  2. The Good Doctor

    I thought the show did a great job of portraying very unusual and interesting medical conditions and, at the same time, introducing us to the main cast of characters. The writers also did not shy away from difficult topics, handling homophobia, drug addiction, homelessness and corporate greed in the health-care system.

  3. A River Runs Through It

    It is a very thorough and meaningful drama that presents a long period of time in a thoughtful and clear way.

  4. True Beauty

    This series is about Lim Ju-kyung, a high school student who always had a complex about her appearance since childhood. To hide her bare face, she started to wear heavy make-up. Her excellent make-up skills enhance her beauty, and persuade others that her beautiful, made-up face is her natural appearance.

  5. Amber Girls School

    is a story of a teenage girl's transformation from timid to confident, as she navigates the challenges of student life, including bullying, academic and peer pressure, teenage romance, physical changes, and mental health.
